Goga Tsutsunava is a 24-year-old digital creator based in Tbilisi, working across design, video editing, and interactive experiences. Alongside his role in IT support, he builds visual projects that explore motion, rhythm, and atmosphere. From minimalist UI concepts to edited video sequences, his work functions as a visual diary — capturing moods, ideas, and moments in time. Currently focused on developing his portfolio, Goga approaches each piece as an experiment, driven by curiosity, fast visual feedback, and a constant search for his own creative voice.

“I want my work to feel like something you miss,

without knowing why.”

My work explores mood, memory, and quiet emotion through design and video. I’m currently focused on learning, experimenting, and building my portfolio — using each project as a way to grow and shape my own visual identity
